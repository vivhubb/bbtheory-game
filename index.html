Game Rules:

You will play against the computer and the first one to achieve 10 points will be the winner of the GAME! Please note that the computer does not cheat! It's selection is completely random!

As Sheldon Cooper explains in the American Sitcom called The Big Bang Theory the game rules are:

"Scissors cuts paper, paper covers rock, rock crushes lizard, lizard poisons Spock, Spock smashes scissors, scissors decapitates lizard, lizard eats paper, paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes rock, and as it always has, rock crushes scissors."
